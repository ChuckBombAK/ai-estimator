SAMPLE 5 — Tricky / unusual material

Repaint a 600 sq ft accent wall and ceiling in a converted barn. Owner wants a
specialty limewash finish (not standard latex) and a metallic gold trim. Three
coats of the limewash to get the texture right. High ceilings — about 16 feet — so
scaffolding or a lift will be needed. Owner: The Hollowbrook Studio.

[Note: non-standard materials (limewash, metallic) won't have the usual unit price;
high ceiling adds an equipment line. Tool should create line items for the unusual
materials and flag the limewash/metallic pricing as an assumption in notes.]
